# Welcome to Micropathology

## What We Do
We provide clinically supported **rapid diagnostics for infections** and **genetic diseases**.  

We also offer a **genetic profiling service for human identification** and conduct **biomedical research** across various aspects of human and veterinary pathology. Our research services include contract research, clinical trials for external organizations, and ongoing in-house assay development.

## Our Standards
We adhere to the highest possible standards, subscribing to **UK and international accreditation**, quality assurance, and best practices to ensure excellence in all our endeavors.  

We welcome ongoing communication with our clients and are happy to discuss how we can help address challenges in infectious disease.

## Our Services
- **Clinical Advice on Infectious Disease**  
  We provide guidance on all aspects of infectious disease management to healthcare colleagues, with no obligation to use our services.

- **Direct LIMS to LIMS Integration**  
  We can collaborate with your IT team to integrate requests and results using HL7 standards.

- **Secure Results Transfer**  
  Results are delivered via HL7 and encrypted PDFs sent securely via email.
